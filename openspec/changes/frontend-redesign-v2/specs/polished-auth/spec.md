## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Atmospheric auth experience
Auth pages SHALL have a warm nature-inspired gradient background with branded logo, title, and subtitle above the form card.

#### Scenario: Auth branding visible
- **WHEN** user is not authenticated
- **THEN** app icon, title, and subtitle SHALL be visible above the login/register card

### Requirement: Refined form inputs
Form inputs SHALL be 44px tall with earth-tone borders and focus states.

#### Scenario: Input focus
- **WHEN** input receives focus
- **THEN** border SHALL transition to primary green with subtle glow
